Resizable Headers

Description:

The Resize Columns feature allows each column to be resized.

Demo:

Drag a the column separator to resize column contents.

The column will obey any minWidth or maxWidth constraints you give it.


Code:

<strong>Drag</strong> a the column separator to resize column contents. <br> <br> The column will obey any <i>minWidth</i> or <i>maxWidth</i> constraints you give it. <br> <br> <div ui-grid="gridOptions" class="grid" ui-grid-resize-columns ui-grid-move-columns></div> <br>
